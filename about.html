Dr. Kailash Prasad Guru Jee is a noted and internationally renowned astrologist and Vastu specialist who is based in Kathmandu, the Nepal capital. In a relatively young age, he has finished his academic degrees in Astrology and Vastu Sastra and has created a name for himself in this field. He is extremely popular not just in Nepal and India, but also in the West where he travels frequently to attend spiritual seminars and conferences and also preaches to his disciples scattered across different parts of the world.
